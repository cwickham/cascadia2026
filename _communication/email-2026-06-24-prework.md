**Subject: See you Friday — Zero to Website with Quarto @ Cascadia R Conference**

Hi everyone,

I'm looking forward to seeing you at **Zero to Website with Quarto** at the Cascadia R Conference this Friday! Everything you will need is on the workshop website: <https://cwickham.github.io/cascadia2026/> 

**Details**

- **When:** Friday, June 26, 2026, 1:30–4:30 PM
- **Where:** Karl Miller Center, 615 SW Harrison Street, Portland, OR

**Please do these four things before we meet:**

1. **Install the latest pre-release of Quarto** (v1.10.\*) from <https://quarto.org/docs/download/prerelease.html>. Bring a laptop and charger.

2. **Pick a publishing destination** so you can put your site online during the workshop:
   - **GitHub Pages** if you're already comfortable creating a repo and pushing to it (no extra setup), or
   - **Posit Connect Cloud** otherwise — sign up for a free account at <https://connect.posit.cloud>.

3. **Gather a little content** to make your site your own: a short paragraph about you (or your site's topic), a photo/image for the home page, and a list of projects, talks, classes, or publications.

4. **Make sure you're comfortable with the Quarto basics** — previewing/rendering documents, and recognizing the YAML header, Markdown, and code cells. If you'd like a refresher, the [Quarto chapter in R for Data Science](https://r4ds.hadley.nz/quarto), the [Getting Started tutorial](https://quarto.org/docs/get-started/hello/rstudio.html), or the [first 16 minutes of this video](https://youtu.be/_f3latmOhew) will get you there.

Full pre-work details are on the [workshop website](https://cwickham.github.io/cascadia2026/), and you can reach me in the `#workshop-quarto-2026` channel on the [conference Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/cascadiarconf/shared_invite/zt-1lu53059t-GAxQtzrwQhmo7BXE7YfC8w) if you hit any snags. Please also review the [Code of Conduct](https://cascadiarconf.com/policies/) for all conference activities.

See you Friday!

Charlotte
